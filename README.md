# 🍫 Chocolate Company Sales Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end Business Intelligence project built using **Power BI** to analyze chocolate sales performance and transform raw business data into actionable insights through interactive dashboards and KPI reporting.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from a chocolate company to uncover trends in revenue, profit, product performance, sales teams, countries, and customer behavior.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify high-performing products and regions, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profitability
- Monitor business KPIs
- Identify top-performing products and salespeople
- Compare country-wise sales performance
- Track monthly sales trends
- Support strategic business decisions using interactive dashboards
